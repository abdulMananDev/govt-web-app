L-G felicitates J&K’s NEET topper

J&K Lieutenant Governor Manoj Sinha on Tuesday met the UT’s NEET topper Basit Bilal Khan at the Raj Bhavan.Applauding efforts of young NEET qualifiers from across the UT, Sinha remarked that he was well aware of the inherent strength of people of the region and the government was committed to providing the requisite environment for their progress.
